pytransform.trajectories.matrices_from_pos_quat¶

pytransform.trajectories.matrices_from_pos_quat(P)[source]¶

Get sequence of homogeneous matrices from positions and quaternions.

Parameters:

P : array-like, shape (n_steps, 7)

Sequence of poses represented by positions and quaternions in the order (x, y, z, w, vx, vy, vz) for each step
Returns:

H : array-like, shape (n_steps, 4, 4)

Sequence of poses represented by homogeneous matrices
