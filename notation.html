Duality of Transformations and Poses¶

We can use a transformation matrix that represents a transformation from frame A to frame B to represent the pose (position and orientation) of frame A in frame B (if we use the active transformation convention; see Transformation Ambiguities for details). This is just a different interpretation of the same matrix and similar to our interpretation of a vector from A to P as a point .
